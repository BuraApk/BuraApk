Welcome to Tesfa Egziabher Academy

directorCEO: Beminet Masresha

The Director of a school plays a pivotal role in shaping the educational environment and ensuring the institution's mission is effectively implemented. This individual is responsible for overseeing all aspects of school operations, including curriculum development, staff management, and student affairs. The Director collaborates with teachers, parents, and the community to foster an inclusive and supportive atmosphere conducive to learning. They are also tasked with strategic planning and budgeting, ensuring that resources are allocated efficiently to enhance educational programs. Additionally, the Director serves as the primary spokesperson for the school, advocating for its needs and achievements within the broader community.
